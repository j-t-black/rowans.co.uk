## Goal
Systematically verify 6 polished pages (home, whats-on, radio, policies, groups, kids) against their Figma Desktop (1920px) frames. Capture side-by-side screenshots and fix any discrepancies found.

- Implementation screenshots at 1920x1080 for comparison
- Fixes applied to any pages with visual discrepancies
